SYSTEM 10: PURE FLOW

Visualizing the Spherical Cap Equilibrium

The Visual Story:

1. Tighter Curve = Higher Pressure. The small bubble pushes its air into the large one.

2. The Hemisphere Spike. The small bubble shrinks until it forms a perfect hemisphere. This is its tightest possible curve, meaning pressure is at maximum.

3. The Flattening. It can't shrink inward anymore, so it flattens into a shallow "contact lens." The curve gets wider until it perfectly matches Bubble A!
